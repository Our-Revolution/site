Local Groups

Our Revolution is made up of 401 local groups from all across the world. With groups in 49 states, Puerto Rico, and 7 countries, the political revolution continues under the leadership of local leaders all across the globe.

Our Revolution groups have elected candidates across the nation, passed ballot initiatives, reorganized their local parties, held candidate trainings and advocated for issues that are driving our nation forward - and we’re just getting started.

Candidates and Ballot Initiatives

Our Revolution is empowering the next generation of progressive leaders by inspiring and supporting candidates to run for office across the entire spectrum of government, as well as endorsing and supporting ballot initiatives across the country.

Over the past year, 75 Our Revolution endorsed candidates and 24 ballot initiatives all over the country have shocked the political establishment. khalid kamau ran a grassroots powered campaign to become South Fulton's next city council member. Christine Pellegrino won in a district where a Republican previously won by 22,000 votes. Chokwe Antar Lumumba won a stunning 93% of the vote to become the Mayor of Jackson, Mississipi. All over the country, inspiring progressive candidates are proving that when we stand together, there's nothing we cannot accomplish.

Our Revolution groups are transforming the Democratic Party.

Over the last year, more than 16,000 people have stepped up to run for Democratic Party leadership in 18 states and 20 Democrats Abroad member countries as a result of our efforts.

We now have allies holding state chair, county chair and other executive positions across the country, and they are actively working to transform the Democratic Party into a party that truly reflects and represents the people in each and every community throughout the U.S.

Progressive Issues

Our Revolution groups have been fighting for progressive issues all across the country - and we’ve been winning.

Right now, a mass coalition of progressive organizations are leading the Summer for Progress, an effort to get House Democrats to endorse the People’s Platform – 8 bills that say definitively people over profit.

Over the past year, citizen leaders have organized sit-ins and rallies across the country to save tha ACA and we’ve pushed for sanctuary community policies all around the country. We’ve fought pipelines and stopped the Trans-Pacific Partnership, and successfully petitioned for the freedom of Oscar Lopez Rivera.
